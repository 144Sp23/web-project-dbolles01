Thanks for visiting my website!

Ocarina of Time is the most influential game I ever played. Growing up, it took me years to actually finish the game, but it created a tremendous sense of exploration and problem solving. Back before internet was as common, us kids would take to the playground in order to discuss how to progress through the game.

This site was created as part of the Web Development I course at Northwest Missouri State University and was developed to learn and enhance the concepts within that course. It is not intended for public use.
