Developed by Nintendo and released in 1998, The Legend of Zelda: Ocarina of Time had a massive playerbase and has sold more than 7 million cartridges by 2000.

Every part of the game has receieved massive appraisal from critics and consumers alike. The game has won several awards for it's visuals, gameplay, and soundtrack.

Because it is such a beloved game, it has seen many rereleases and constant availability on almost all nintendo consoles moving forward.
